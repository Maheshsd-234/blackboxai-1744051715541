BlockHealth in Action

Practical applications that demonstrate the platform's value across healthcare situations.

New Doctor Visit

Visiting a new specialist? Grant them temporary access to your complete medical history with one click.

How BlockHealth Helps:

  • No need to fill out lengthy medical history forms
  • Doctor sees verified records, not patient recollections
  • Access automatically expires after visit

Second Opinion

Seeking a second opinion from a specialist? Share your scans and test results securely.

How BlockHealth Helps:

  • No need to physically transfer files or CDs
  • Specialist can view original, unaltered reports
  • Revoke access when consultation is complete

Global Telemedicine

Consulting with doctors across borders? Share records internationally with no security risks.

How BlockHealth Helps:

  • No jurisdictional data transfer issues
  • Doctor can view but not download records
  • Automatic translation of standardized medical codes

Emergency Situation

Unconscious or unable to communicate? Paramedics can access critical information instantly.

How BlockHealth Helps:

  • Emergency toggle grants temporary access
  • Shows only critical info (allergies, medications, conditions)
  • Access logged and automatically revoked after crisis

Emergency Access When Seconds Matter

BlockHealth's emergency mode gives first responders instant access to critical health information while maintaining security.

Learn about emergency features

How It Works:

  • QR Code on phone/watch/ID card links to your profile

  • Limited Access shows only critical medical information

  • Notifications alert you when emergency access is used
